Choo Lo - The Local Train (Cover Song)

Music Cover

  • Skills: Vocalist, Guitarist

  • Project URL: CHOO LO - THE LOCAL TRAIN (OCTAVIUM COVER)

An indie song with the dash of soul and the groove of soft rock combined with the eccentricity of TLT's style, Choo Lo never fails to tug on your heartstrings. TLT has always been close to the band since it came onto the Indian music scene and this is our way of showing our love for one of our utmost favorite bands. Heart touching lyrics, tasteful licks, moving melody and a mesmerizing aura makes this one a song for the ages.
